Helped Dan with oyster larval setting. Went to Taylor Shellfish Hatchery in Quilcene, WA with Kristin Saksa to collect and count larvae. After larvae were collected, counted and incubated (refrigerator for 1hr), we transported the larvae to the Manchester facility.

At Manchester facility, Kristin and I labelled larval down-wellers (silos), assisted with initiation of setting (using epinephrine), washing of larvae, assembly of silos within totes, counting/sampling of larvae for molecular biological work, and distribution of larvae.

This was a very long day. Caught the 6:30AM ferry to Bainbridge and caught the 12:30AM ferry from Southworth (Manchester) to Fauntleroy!
